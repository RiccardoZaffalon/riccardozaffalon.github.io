About

A big passion of mine since my teenage years, in 2014 I started developing websites professionally for clients near me.

Across 2015 and 2016 I was a full-time intern at Webtechnet, near Venice, where I worked on frontend and WordPress development.

I then moved to Canada to improve my understanding of the industry and the complete production process, while enhancing my capability to work with diverse clients from different parts of the world.

Currently, and since the beginning of 2018, I help improving online shopping experiences at alpenite, where I develop and provide consultancy on Salesforce Commerce Cloud projects for recognized fashion brands such as PINKO, Cisalfa Sport, and Brunello Cucinelli.

In my current role, I strive to bring value to my team by improving the production process with my experience and earned skills, introducing new technologies, and easing the onboarding of junior colleagues, whom I then aim to help grow and mentor.
